GOVERNMENT ENGINEERING COLLEGE PALAKKAD

The Government Engineering College, Palakkad (GEC Palakkad) is a public engineering college located in Sreekrishnapuram, Palakkad district, Kerala, India. It was established in 1999 and is affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (formerly Kerala Technological University). The college offers Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) programs in various engineering disciplines. The campus is situated in a rural setting and has basic infrastructure including laboratories, library, and other academic facilities. The institution operates under the Department of Technical Education, Government of Kerala.
